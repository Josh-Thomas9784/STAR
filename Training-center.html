Inside the Center

Here at STAR, Our training centers are built to the highest level of perfection acheivable. We build our centers around the astrounaunts training here at STAR, making Training centers suitable for any person wanting to become an astrounaunt. Currently we have the Main training center built into the STAR center as well as the Vortex Center.

Main Training Center

Our main center is available to newcomers, who want to learn the basics of becoming an astronaunt. Built into the STAR center, the Main Training Center is the most versitle center we have, ranging from education of space to high intensity physical training.

Vortex Center

The Vortex Center is built for the more advanced astrounaunts with us. Located 5 miles from the main STAR compound, the Vortex Center is easily accesiable to the members of the STAR organization. The Vortex Center also has the pressure and gravity chamber which allow astronaunts to be accostomed to the enviornment of space.

A New Center?

In 2024, STAR is planning to make another Training Center to mainly focus on the controlling and flight of rocketships. Here at STAR, we wanted to bring a center that will handle all test launches and account for the safety of our astronaunts. So in 2024, we will build the Rocket Center, located near the base compound and accesiable to all STAR members.
