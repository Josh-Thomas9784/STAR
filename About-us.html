Our Goal

The main goal here at the Space Training and Retreat Center (STAR) is to make it possible for people to go and travel around space and fulfill their life long dreams. We seek to give opportunity to those who want an out of this world adventure. Our purpose is to provide a unique travel destination with incredible sights and relaxation and to help people become astronauts and visit space.

At STAR we train those who want to be astronauts. In the STAR center, we use our advanced technology to prepare astronauts and make a safer environment for those who travel to space. We are constantly making advances in space technology and Launches.

Origins

Mark Huckleburg (our CEO) began with the idea of a space outreach program in his 4th year at Rice University. There he made a ground breaking discovery in space technology with the help of co-founders: Bill Fates and Steve Hobs. After graduating from Rice University, the three founders procceded to build upon the idea from Bill's basement

This idea was then made into the Space Training and Retreat program (also known as STAR). The founders decided to help others achieve their dreams to reach space and shifted their idea into the very company we have now.
